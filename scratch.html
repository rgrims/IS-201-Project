Plan Your Perfect Vacation

My favorite countries:

  1. Spain
    • An incredibly scenic place. Architecture and housing style is truly beautiful.
    • Rich culture and even richer tasting food!

  2. Philippines
    • Make sure to check out Batangas, Philippines for one of the biggest coral reefs in the world!
    • If you are looking for a truly unique experience, this is it! Such beautiful scenery!
    • However, it is a 3rd world country, so be ready for it.

  3. Guatemala
    • One of my all-time favorite places! The people are the nicest and most humble you will ever meet.
    • This is the place for you if you like outdoor activities. It has several large lakes and an incredible amount of hikable volcanoes.

  4. Ireland and Scotland
